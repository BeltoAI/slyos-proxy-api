FAQs

Is this crypto?

No. SLY are platform usage credits—no coins.

What workloads?

Embeddings, lightweight inference, bursty evals first; expanding via device-class routing.

How do I trust results?

Receipts include replication %, consensus details, anomalies, and timings.

Can I restrict where jobs run?

Yes—private pools or vetted contributor pools with policy controls.

What about SLAs?

We measure validated completion time, not just raw device speed.
